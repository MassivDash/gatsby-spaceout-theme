# Children kids health center

This wordpress built site, was designed by spaceout.pl for Warsaw private kids health center.

The website is mainly designed in the material design approach, with concerned mothers in mind, as they are most likely to be the end user of this website.

The owners except for kid friendly look also wanted to be able to move all of their questioners, tranning submissions and other various paperwork from offline to online. As their budget was tight and not really in range of custom web app, the clear choice was the wordpress installation.
