# Flotowe Trendy, custom wordpress site, taking in submmissions for car managment fleet confernece

Owner of this yearly conference as most people these days, wanted to move his offline paperwork into the online world. The focus was to move conference submissions into the digital world, provide aggresive "car" look, and build up email subscribers list.

One of the troubles you may hit with a wordpress installations and "special offer" server deals is that, is that cheap servers are not accustomed to big ways of from submissions. Owners' cheap server was not able to withstand sudden ways of vistors after emails with invitation were sent.
